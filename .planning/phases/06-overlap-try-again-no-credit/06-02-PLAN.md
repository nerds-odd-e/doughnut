<objective>
Honor D-04 durably: persist `Answer.outcome` via Flyway (RESEARCH preferred / yolo default) and exclude OVERLAP from `countWrongAnswersSinceForMemoryTracker` so repeated try-agains cannot trip re-assimilation.

Output: Migration V300000236+, persisted outcome, threshold exclusion proof.
</objective>
